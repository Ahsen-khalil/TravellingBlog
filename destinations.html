Zurich

Welcome to Zurich , the beautiful city of switzerland. Here, you can explore beautiful landscapes and enjoy a variety of activities. Some must-visit places include:

  • Bridge City River
  • Crowne Plaza Zurich
  • Fifa Museum

Local Tips

  • Traffic Tips: Buy a 24-hours ticket for public transportation since it pays off already with your second ride.
  • A place to rest your head and not break the bank: Zurich Youth Hostel, a modern building and a good breakfast.
