A beautiful Lake

Lake Oeschinen, located in the Bernese Oberland near Kandersteg, is considered one of the most stunning lakes in all of Switzerland. And rightly so. Framed by the peaks of Bluemlisalp and Doldernhorn, Lake Oeschinen (Oeschinensee in German) is filled with exceptionally clear turquoise water.
